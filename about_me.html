About Me

I'm a self-employed software developer experienced in coding and designing web applications living in Turkiye, loving programming, especially dynamic languages like Python, Javascript. Besides my programming fascination, I am highly interested in history, economics, camping and trekking.

Let's start from the beginning of the story.

I was born in a small town named Kalamaki in the south of the Anatolia, on June of 1987, as a son of two young teachers. At my first 10 year in the world was not so much interesting, I was an ordinary kid loving playing Atari games like Mortal Combat and similar legendary games.

Education

My introduction to programming started when I was in an IMF supported laboratuary middle-school by I wanted to learn HTML. This learning process was one of my major reasons to continue high school in Computer Software department of an Anatolian Technical High School. I took Basic Electronics, Algorithms and similar programming lessons by coding Pascal and Visual Basic in this school. After the high school, I introduced to C, Delphi and C# in Computer Programming department of S. Demirel University.

Open Source

The first open source project I developed was a javascript library named "Civciv" which means "chick" in Turkish. I documented this project in Turkish and used in development of a multiplayer connect4 application. My second experiment was better, it was a web-based development environment named Rainbow9. This project isn't maintained anymore, too, but can be found at its project page. In 2008, PI.JS and a multiplayer chess application followed these first steps. To get more details about the projects I'm currently working on, you may check out the Projects page. Also, there is a web page listing my old/deprecated projects.

Work

After I graduated from the first college I studied at (Computer Programming), started to work as Javascript Coder for a company in Farnington called Pagos Inc. developing a web based spreadsheets application. Once I was working this job, I introduced to comet technichs and released a library named pi.comet which was the major reason of that eBay Turkey hired me.By working for eBay Turkey, I got a chance to focus on the open source projects I was interested at, Firebug Lite was one of them. On the beginning of the December 2009, I left this job and started to work for another company called Parkyeri to develop myself at Python and PostgreSQL. You may check out my resume to get more information about my professional experience.

Currently, I'm working for just my personal projects and trying to improve my self in programming and some various topics like economics, history. Also, travelling to the places I discover using Google Maps is one of my passions and classic rock is my favourite music gender but I listen every kind of music except sound loops like car alerts :) You may check out my profiles in the websites listed below in this page, to get more info about me. Feel free to be participated in the projects I'm developing and get in touch about any topics I'm interested, as well.Thank you for reading.
